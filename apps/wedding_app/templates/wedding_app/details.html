Directions

If you are flying in, we recommend you fly in to Sacramento International Airport, as it is only 1 hr 45 mins from the venue. You can also fly into Oakland International Airport, which is just over 2 hours away. Murphys is approximately 2.5 hours from San Jose.


Parking

Free public parking is available across the street at the Murphys Creek Theater. Once parked, on exiting the parking lot, turn right and continue approximately 50 feet. Venue will be on your left.

Venue Rules

The pool is off limits. Please do not enter the pool unless you would like to be thrown out. We don’t want to have you thrown out. And we certainly don’t want to have to ask you to pay the associated fine. But we will.

Smoking and vaping on the premises will only be allowed in designated areas. This includes cigarettes, cigars, pipes, e-cigarettes, vape-pens, and anything in-between. And please properly dispose of your cigarette butts, there will be containers.SCOTT!!

Sorry, people - no outside alcohol. There will be a bartender serving free wine and beer, so have at ours.
